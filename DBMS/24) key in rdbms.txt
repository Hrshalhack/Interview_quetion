*key-> it is used to uniquely identify the row(tuple).A key is also used when
 we want to establish relationships between the different columns and tables.

*types of keys-> 

1)super key-> 
2)candidate key-> 
3)primarykey-> it has certain set of rules->1.there should be only one primary
              key in table.
              2.it should be unique.
              3.it cannot be null.
4)foregn key-> common column called foregnkey,to connect two tables.it can be null.
