#when use SQL->

*if your data is primarily sturctured,a SQL db is the right choice.
*SQL db is the best suited for transaction-oriented system such as customer
 relationship management tools,accounting software and e-commerce platforms.
*if your data is very structured and ACID is must, the SQL is great choice.

#when use NoSQL-> 

*if your data requirements aren't clear or if your data is unstructured.
 NoSQL may be your best choice.
*in NoSQL db you do not need a predefined schema like you do for a SQL db.
*A NoSQL db is much better fir to store data like article content,social media
post, sesor data.
*NoSQL db are more scalable and flexible.
